Cornet Bay Retreat Center

The Cornet Bay Retreat Center sits within Deception Pass State Park, one of the most scenic parks in Washington. The retreat center was built in the first half of the 20th century as part of the Civilian Conservation Corps. Cornet Bay retains much of its rich history by maintaining original buildings, hiking trails and vistas.

Our wedding weekend will take place entirely at the retreat center and we welcome all guests to stay with us Friday and Saturday in the rustic summer-camp style cabins and bunkhouses. The park has many hiking trails, picnic areas, and playing fields. We hope to spend the weekend playing games and reuniting with friends and family as we celebrate our wedding.

Cabins

This venue includes many sleeping cabins which we are able to provide to our guests. If you've ever been to an overnight summer camp, you'll know what to expect. Every cabin has close access to a restroom with showers, and are within close walking distance to the rest of the venue.

If you are interested in having your group stay in a cabin, please fill out the survey form below!

Location & Directions

There are two main ways to get to the venue from Seattle & SeaTac International. First is taking I-5 to I-20 at Mt. Vernon. This route takes you over the scenic Deception Pass bridge and through most of the park. The second was is taking the Mukilteo-Clinton ferry to Whidbey Island, and driving north on I-20 to the park. This route includes a very scenic ferry ride as well as a full tour of Whidbey Island. However, in the summer months the ferry wait time can be up to 3 hours, so plan accordingly.

If you are interested in taking a shuttle to and from downtown Seattle to the venue, please fill out the survey below.

Whidbey & Fidalgo Island

Of course, there’s plenty to do outside of the camp – Deception Pass State Park spans the strait between Whidbey and Fidalgo islands, both of which are populated by many small towns with places to eat, shop and adventure.

We know that cabin camping is not everyone’s idea of fun, so we’ve scoped out a few other options in nearby Anacortes.

The Majestic Inn & Spa

Right in the heart of downtown Anacortes, The Majestic Inn provides a luxurious hotel experience in a historic venue.

Sunrise Inn Villa & Suites

One block off Main Street, the Sunrise Inn Villas & Suites provide newly remodeled suites as well as two villas available for families at a mid-range price.

Anaco Bay Inn

A traditional seaside style hotel, the Anaco Bay Inn is a great option for families and groups. The hotel offers traditional rooms as well as fully-furnished apartments at a very reasonable rate.
